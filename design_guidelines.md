# Curators Community Design Guidelines

## Design Philosophy
A clean, elegant digital art community website featuring a sophisticated black and gold aesthetic. The design emphasizes simplicity, readability, and a welcoming atmosphere for video editors and content creators.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Pure Black** (#000000 / rgb(0, 0, 0)) - Main backgrounds, creating depth and sophistication
- **Warm Gold** (#D4AF37 / rgb(212, 175, 55)) - Primary accents, buttons, highlights, brand elements
- **Soft White** (#FFFFFF / rgb(255, 255, 255)) - Primary text on dark backgrounds

### Secondary Colors
- **Charcoal Gray** (#1A1A1A / rgb(26, 26, 26)) - Card backgrounds, elevated surfaces
- **Medium Gray** (#808080 / rgb(128, 128, 128)) - Secondary text, muted elements
- **Light Gray** (#B0B0B0 / rgb(176, 176, 176)) - Tertiary text, subtle borders

### Usage Guidelines
- **Backgrounds**: Pure black for main areas, charcoal for cards/sections
- **Text**: White for headings, light gray for body text, medium gray for captions
- **Accents**: Warm gold for CTAs, highlights, borders, brand elements
- **NO navy blue or burgundy/red colors** - keep palette minimal and elegant

## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) - elegant, sophisticated
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, highly readable
- **Accents**: Montserrat (sans-serif) - buttons and CTAs

### Hierarchy
- **H1**: 3.5rem - 4.5rem, Playfair Display, Gold or White
- **H2**: 2.5rem - 3rem, Playfair Display, Gold or White
- **H3**: 1.75rem - 2rem, Playfair Display, White
- **Body**: 1rem - 1.125rem, Inter, Light Gray
- **Small**: 0.875rem, Inter, Medium Gray

## Spacing System
- **Small**: 0.5rem - 1rem
- **Medium**: 1.5rem - 2rem
- **Large**: 3rem - 4rem
- **XL**: 5rem - 6rem

Consistent spacing creates visual rhythm and hierarchy.

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Gold background, black text, medium padding
- **Secondary**: Gold border, gold text, transparent background
- **Sizes**: Consistent height (min-h-12 for standard buttons)
- **Hover**: Subtle brightness increase, smooth transitions

### Cards
- Charcoal background (#1A1A1A)
- Subtle border or no border (minimal design)
- Medium padding (1.5rem - 2rem)
- Hover: Subtle elevation or brightness change

### Sections
- Full-width layouts
- Generous vertical padding (4rem - 6rem)
- Clean separation with subtle dividers or spacing
- Background: Pure black or slight gradient overlay on gallery photo

## Background & Imagery

### Gallery Photo Background
- Use the authentic art gallery photo (`file_00000000f12c61f8bc6a0ea3182dd5c1_1761564979013.png`) as the main background
- Apply dark overlay (black with 50-70% opacity) to ensure text readability
- Use CSS `background-size: cover` and `background-position: center`
- Optional: Parallax scrolling effect for depth

### Image Treatment
- NO ornate frames or borders around images
- Clean, simple presentation
- Subtle shadows or glows for depth (optional)
- Maintain aspect ratios

## Layout Principles

### Single-Scroll Landing Page
1. **Hero Section**: Large, impactful with gallery photo background
2. **Value Propositions**: 3-column grid showcasing community benefits
3. **Community Highlights**: Testimonials, metrics, or member spotlights
4. **Championship/Events**: Call-to-action for upcoming events
5. **Join Section**: Newsletter signup or Discord invitation
6. **Footer**: Social links, copyright, minimal information

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- Stack columns on mobile
- Maintain generous spacing
- Ensure buttons and text remain readable

## Interactions & Animations

### Hover Effects
- Subtle brightness or opacity changes
- Smooth transitions (300-500ms)
- NO dramatic transformations or layout shifts

### Scroll Animations
- Fade-in effects for sections (optional)
- Parallax on background (subtle)
- Smooth scrolling behavior

### Accessibility
- Maintain AA contrast ratios (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text)
- Ensure golden accents on black meet contrast requirements
- Provide focus states for keyboard navigation

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional yet welcoming
- Inspiring and aspirational
- Community-focused
- Encouraging creativity and collaboration

### Messaging
- Emphasize community, learning, and growth
- Highlight the creative process and storytelling
- Celebrate digital artistry and editing craft
- Invite participation and connection

## Don'ts
- ❌ NO navy blue backgrounds
- ❌ NO burgundy/red accents
- ❌ NO ornate frames or museum-style gallery presentation
- ❌ NO overly complex layouts
- ❌ NO cluttered sections

## Do's
- ✅ Keep it simple and clean
- ✅ Use black and gold exclusively for brand colors
- ✅ Focus on community and creativity
- ✅ Maintain elegant, sophisticated aesthetic
- ✅ Ensure excellent readability
- ✅ Use the gallery photo as an atmospheric background
