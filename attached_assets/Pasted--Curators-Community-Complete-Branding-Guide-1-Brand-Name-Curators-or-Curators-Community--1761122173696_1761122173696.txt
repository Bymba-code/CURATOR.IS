
Curators Community — Complete Branding Guide

1. Brand Name
Curators (or Curators Community)

2. Brand Quote
“We dream our paint and we paint our dream.” — Vincent van Gogh

3. Slogan
Edit mind, not video.

4. Brand Purpose / Mission
Curators is a creative movement and community uniting all who shape perception through storytelling, editing, and artistic expression in the digital age. We believe creativity is born within — it cannot be taught, only discovered and nurtured by the creator.
Our mission is to gather the top screen creatives, unite them, and empower them to edit the world’s mind like a Чингисийн нууц товчоо. No matter your background, age, or location — if you have a device, you’re a mind editor with creativity. Join us and be part of the 21st-century artistic revolution.

5. Brand Values
	• Creativity First: Creativity is the foundation of all we do.
	• Support & Growth: We foster a supportive space for learning and pushing boundaries.
	• Respect: Every voice and journey is honored.
	• Professionalism: We help members become masters of their craft.
	• Inclusivity: Everyone with creative passion is welcome.

6. Brand Voice & Tone
	• Knowledgeable, like an art connoisseur guiding fellow artists.
	• Friendly and approachable, never intimidating.
	• Noble and inspiring, uplifting every member’s creative spirit.
	• Thoughtful and clear, blending art and mindset coaching.

	 Concept
	• Minimalist, flat design style.
	• Visual of a modern art gallery wall with several simple rectangular frames arranged neatly.
	• Some frames may contain subtle icons like a play button or a brush stroke to symbolize video editing and creativity.
	• Thin line art, clean, airy, balanced asymmetry for a tasteful, modern look.

8. Messaging Framework
Elevator Pitch
Curators is a creative community where storytelling and editing meet artistic expression. We believe creativity is innate, discovered by the individual. Our mission is to unite digital creators from around the world and empower them to edit not just videos, but minds — shaping the future of art in the 21st century.
Key Benefits
	• Learn to harness your creativity beyond just editing tools.
	• Connect with top creatives for collaboration and feedback.
	• Access exclusive tutorials, challenges, and resources.
	• Gain opportunities to monetize your editing and storytelling skills.
	• Be part of a supportive, inclusive community that respects your journey.
Taglines & Slogans
	• Edit mind, not video.

9. Community Culture & Rules
	• Creativity is the first priority.
	• Be supportive, provide constructive feedback.
	• Respect all members and their creative paths.
	• Embrace ambition and continuous learning.
	• Foster a safe and open space for honest expression.

10. Content & Engagement Strategy (for your team/devs)
	• Regular creative challenges and edit battles.
	• Weekly tutorials, tips, and mindset coaching posts.
	• Highlight member success stories and case studies.
	• Host live Q&A and workshops.
	• Encourage member collaboration through channels or groups.

11. Sample Social Media Bio
Curators is a global community of digital artists and editors shaping stories and minds. We believe creativity is born within. Join us to learn, create, and edit the 21st-century world. #EditMindNotVideo

12. Website Key Sections (suggested)
	• Home: Mission, slogan, call to join.
	• About: Brand story, values, vision.
	• Community: Membership benefits, rules, testimonials.
	• Resources: Tutorials, challenges, articles.
	• Events: Workshops, live streams, contests.
	• Contact: Join links, socials, support.
